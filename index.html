    Guide

    Train yourself playing your instrument by ear.

    Click on "play" to start the melody, then try reproducing with you instrument.

    Once succeeded, try another excerpt by clicking "next".

    If your instrument is not in concert pitch, you can change it in the transpose input. (Example "Bb3" for Bb instruments).

    You can set the range of your instrument by changing the lowest and highest notes. You can refer to: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_pitch_notation for pitch names.
