Credits

    Project by Benjamin Devillers <bdvllrs>.

    Music excerpts are from this dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/soumikrakshit/classical-music-midi.

    This uses the following tools:

    • Tone.js for sound synthesizer.
    • vexflow to generate music score.
    • fontawesome for the icons.
    • MIDI.js for the piano soundfont.
    • peterkhayes/pitchfinder for the pitch detection.
